The New Way to become an Office Manager

Applications are hereby invited from interested citizens of this world, for admission into membership of International Institute of Church Office Management.

Be a member

Categories of Membership

The Institute Membership is of three (3) categories:


  • Professional Church office Manager: Must be a candidate who successfully completed the professional examination of the institute. Such person has the right to inscribe the designatory acronym of “PCOM” after his/her name.
  • Fellow Professional church office Manager: A fellow applicant must have stayed for 10 years in professional status of the institute right/privilege to inscribe the mandatory acronym “FCOM” after his/her name.
  • Honorary Fellowship: Conferred on promoters, church administrator, church secretary, church worker, and church officer with lot of experience on this job. The institute is aware that there are people who are doing remarkable work in church office management activities both in Nigeria and beyond. Base on this, the institute will offer it Honorary Fellowship grade award to any desirous candidate with great exceptional contribution to field of church administration and management.
